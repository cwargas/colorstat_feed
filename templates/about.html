A better way to track the news

Colorstat delivers a superior way of keeping up with breaking news events. We link to many of the major news outlets so you'll never miss an important story. Links are shown to encourage the reader to visit the original source. A disturbing trend we see is news outlets bombarding readers with pop-up ads and auto-play videos making for a terrifying user experience. Colorstat was created to have all breaking news headlines at your fingertips. You decide what you want to read more of.
